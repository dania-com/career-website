Step-by-Step Path

  1. Love Animals: Have a strong passion for helping and caring for animals of all kinds.
  2. Do Well in School: Focus on science subjects like Biology, Chemistry, and Math.
  3. Graduate Secondary School: Achieve good grades and apply to a university with veterinary programs.
  4. Study Veterinary Medicine: Complete a veterinary degree (usually 5-6 years).
  5. Internship & Practice: Work with real animals during internships or clinic training.
  6. Pass the Exam: Take the national licensing exam to officially become a veterinarian.
  7. Start Your Career: Work at a clinic, animal shelter, zoo, or even open your own vet practice!
